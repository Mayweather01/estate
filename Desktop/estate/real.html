Good to know

How long does land registration take? +
Typically 4–8 weeks depending on government processing and the completeness of submitted documents.
Do you help with tenant screening? +
Yes. We verify identity, employment/income, references and payment history before signing a lease.
Can you design commercial spaces? +
Absolutely. Our architects deliver retail, office and hospitality spaces that optimise circulation, compliance and brand.
Do you offer guaranteed rent? +
Yes, under our lease-to-us model we pay a fixed amount monthly for the term, and we handle tenants and maintenance.
